Mexican Pizza

Description:

Homemade Mexican Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 can refried beans
  • 3 flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatos, diced
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced jalapeno pepper
  • 1/4 cup sour cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C.
  2. Place ground beaf, onion and garlic in a skillet over medum heat. Cook 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease. Season beef with chilli powder, cumin, paprika, salt and pepper.
  3. Lay one tortilla in each pie plate and cover with a layer of refried beans. Spread 1/2 of the ground beef over each one and then cover with a second tortilla. Bake in the preheater oven until tortillas are crisp, about 10 minutes.
  4. REmove pizzas from the oven and set a side to cool slightly. Then spread 1/2 of the salsa over each top tortilla. Cover each pizza with 1/2 of the Cheddar and Monteray Jack cheeses. Place 1/2 of the tomatoes, 1/2 of the green onions, and 1/2 of the jalapeno slices onto each one.
  5. Return pizzas to the oven and bake until cheese is melted.
