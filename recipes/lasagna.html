Homemade Lasagna

Description:

Special homemade lasagna with tomato sauce and cheesy filling. Serve with greany leaf salad and crusty garlic bread

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • diced onion
  • canned tomatoes
  • two tablespoons of parsley and one clove of garlic
  • dried basil , dried origano, salt and black pepper
  • uncooked noodles
  • cottage cheese, parmesan and shredded mozzarella
  • eggs

Steps

  1. cook the meat
  2. cook the noodles
  3. make the cheese layer
  4. assamble the lasagna
  5. bake the lasagna
